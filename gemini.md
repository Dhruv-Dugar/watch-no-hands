# Gemini Prompt Guide: L'Absence Assets

The following are highly detailed image generation prompts for "L'Absence", a luxury brand for an ultra-premium watch that has **no hands, no numbers, and no time indicators**. The target audience is the extreme elite (billionaires) who command time rather than track it.

## The Vibe & Aesthetic
- **Color Palette:** Deep, pitch black (Vantablack, Obsidian, Jet Black), accented by subtle, expensive materials like brushed platinum, champagne gold, and dark Damascus steel.
- **Lighting:** Cinematic, dramatic, single-source studio lighting. Low-key lighting with sharp drop-off shadows to emphasize shape, texture, and mystery.
- **Photography Style:** Macro product photography, photorealistic, 8k resolution, shot on a Hasselblad or medium format camera. Shallow depth of field where appropriate.
- **Brand Feeling:** Hermès waitlist, Rolex exclusivity, Porsche 911 timelessness, quiet money.

---

## Asset Prompts

### 1. The Hero Image / Motion Graphic Placeholder
**Prompt:** 
> A photorealistic, hyper-detailed, dramatic studio shot of a perfectly circular luxury watch face. The watch has absolutely no hands, no numbers, and no dials—just a flawless, unbroken disc of polished black obsidian encased in a thin bezel of brushed platinum. The lighting is cinematic and moody: a single sharp beam of light sweeps across the edge, creating a brilliant reflection on the metal while the rest fades into a pure vantablack background. Exudes extreme wealth, minimalism, and quiet power. 8k, medium format photography, perfect symmetry.

### 2. The Philosophy Section Graphic
**Prompt:**
> A conceptual, abstract luxury 3D rendering. A single, perfect, highly polished dark sphere resting on a slab of rough, matte black slate. A razor-thin ring of champagne gold floats just above the sphere. The lighting is stark and dramatic, casting long, deep shadows. The image should convey the concept of "time standing completely still." Photorealistic, minimalist, extremely high-end, monochrome with a single touch of gold.

### 3. Carousel: Model 1 - "The Absolute"
**Prompt:**
> A photorealistic macro shot of a sleek circular platinum watch with no hands. The dial is made of deep, pure black jade. The camera angle is slightly off-center to catch a soft, luxurious reflection on the crystal. Minimalist, expensive, dark luxury. The background is pure black.

### 4. Carousel: Model 2 - "The Void"
**Prompt:**
> A photorealistic macro shot of a luxury watch made of dark folded Damascus steel. The dial is pure, reflective black obsidian with absolutely no hands or markers. Vantablack background. Cinematic rim lighting highlights the intricate folds of the steel. 

### 5. Carousel: Model 3 - "The Monolith"
**Prompt:**
> A dramatic, photorealistic macro shot of a brushed satin 18ct gold watch with no hands. The dial features a dark, textured raw meteorite pattern. Cinematic lighting casts deep shadows, emphasizing the weight and texture of the meteorite against the smooth matte gold. Pure black background.

### 6. Carousel: Model 4 - "The Zenith"
**Prompt:**
> A sleek, ultra-minimalist matte titanium watch with absolutely no hands or markers. The face is a void—so dark it looks like a black hole absorbing light. The watch appears to be floating weightless in a dark, empty void. High contrast, sharp edges, futuristic yet timeless luxury.
